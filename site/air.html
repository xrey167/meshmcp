Air is the AirDrop-native face of meshmcp. Discover who's on your mesh, drop a file, push a snippet, fetch by hash, approve a held call — every transfer between cryptographic identities on a dark mesh: no cloud, no accounts, no open ports. Resumable across a roam, policy-gated, and provable in a tamper-evident ledger.

  • discoverwho's reachable — each row a WireGuard identity
  • dropfiles, E2E-encrypted & content-hash audited
  • pushclipboard or a task to a peer's inbox
  • fetcha blob by content hash, zero-exposure
  • steerdrive an agent, session, or task — send · cancel · nudge · broadcast
  • launchspawn a new agent or workflow on the mesh
  • approveco-sign a held call — the phone is the human
