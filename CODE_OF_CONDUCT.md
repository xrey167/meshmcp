# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, critique code and ideas rather
than people, and keep the space welcoming to newcomers regardless of who they
are. Harassment, personal attacks, and sustained disruption are not tolerated.

Reports: use the private security/maintainer contact in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — conduct reports are handled confidentially by the
maintainer. Consequences follow the Contributor Covenant's enforcement
guidelines, from a private warning up to a permanent ban.
